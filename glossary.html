Imperial Glossary

Terms, offices, and phrases used in the Karnivale archive.

Dark Amarr

A common name for the Khanid Kingdom, reflecting its fusion of Amarrian tradition with darker aesthetics and Caldari-inspired pragmatism.

Cyberknight

A rare Khanid archetype associated with augmentation, combat training, and discipline. Within the Karnivale, an honorary title for technical specialists.

Flagellant

A holy-path pilot whose devotion is expressed through pilgrimage, suffering, obedience, and battlefield zeal.

Templar

A chaplain, advisor, and doctrinal enforcer who safeguards the Chapter’s spiritual discipline and chain of command.

KK

The vessel prefix used by Khanid Karnivale, interpreted as King Khanid’s.

Ringmaster

The Chapter Master and final authority of Khanid Karnivale. The office is currently held by Paladin Crusader, Patriot Cela Kashuken.

Blackguard Hulls

Elite Chapter vessels kept beyond normal Loyalist access.

Kafir

An in-character term used by extremists within the Chapter for enemies of the faith.
