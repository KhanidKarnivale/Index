The Arsenal of the Kingdom

Khanid Ships

Black and silver hulls, missile doctrine, armor, and aristocratic menace.

Many T2 Amarr ships are of Khanid origin. Unlike most Amarr ships, these designs are known for missiles rather than lasers, giving them a distinct battlefield identity.
