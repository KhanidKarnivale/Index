Why We Fly Khanid Ships

Khanid ships are contradictions made useful: Amarrian armor, Caldari missile doctrine, aristocratic menace, and practical violence.

We fly them because they look like a threat before the first launcher cycles, and because a beautiful ship makes even a loss worth remembering.
