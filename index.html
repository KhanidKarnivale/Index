About Us

Khanid Karnivale is a casual but committed faction warfare corporation operating in the Amarr Empire through the proud traditions of the Khanid Kingdom.

We are a collection of independent pilots, veteran capsuleers, and new voices alike—united by our love of good fights, stylish ships, and victory on our own terms.

We do not just fly for the Empire. We fly for the Kingdom.

The Khanid Way

The Khanid Kingdom broke away from the Empire centuries ago and forged its own path. We embrace that same philosophy in New Eden.

  • Independent thinking
  • Self-sufficiency
  • Competence over ego
  • Style over efficiency, within reason
  • Good fights over meaningless stats

If you are going to explode, you might as well do it looking magnificent.

Theme & Style

We prioritize the Khanid aesthetic. Whether it is flying Khanid hulls, using Khanid SKINs, or bringing dark imperial elegance to the battlefield—style matters.

  • Khanid ships and designs
  • Khanid SKINs whenever possible
  • Dark, refined, and imperial
  • Make your ship a statement
