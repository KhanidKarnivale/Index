The Dark Amarr

An Empire of Faith, Adaptation, and Silvered Steel

The Khanid Kingdom still upholds many traditions of Amarr society, but it has also embraced the practical customs of others, most notably the Caldari. Visitors often describe it as a surreal fusion of Amarr grandeur and Caldari utility.

That contradiction is the point. Tradition without adaptation becomes dust. Adaptation without identity becomes noise.
