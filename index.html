Who We Are

Khanid Karnivale is a relaxed but purposeful faction warfare corporation fighting for the Amarr Empire through a distinctly Khanid lens.


We value camaraderie, memorable fights, beautiful ships, and pilots who enjoy New Eden without treating it like a second job.


Fly dangerous. Fly beautiful. Fly Khanid.

The Khanid Way

The Khanid Kingdom forged its own path through independence, discipline, and military strength.


We embrace the same philosophy:


  • Independent thinking
  • Self-sufficiency
  • Competence over ego
  • Good fights over killboard obsession
  • Style over efficiency (within reason)

Because if you're going to explode, you might as well explode looking fantastic.
