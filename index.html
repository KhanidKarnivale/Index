The Dark Amarr

Faith, Adaptation, and Silvered Steel

The Kingdom retained Amarr hierarchy, ritual, faith, and grandeur while embracing Caldari pragmatism, efficiency, and technological appetite. Tradition without adaptation becomes dust. Adaptation without identity becomes noise.

The Karnivale exists in that contradiction: reverent enough to kneel, independent enough to reload.
