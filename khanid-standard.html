The Khanid Standard

Appearance is not vanity. It is identity made visible. Every black-and-silver hull, every emerald highlight, every Cyber Knight skin tells New Eden that the Kingdom still has teeth.

Directive to New Pilots

Fly the Kingdom When Practical

As new pilots join the Chapter, KK capsuleers are encouraged to fly Khanid ships or Khanid-themed SKINs whenever practical. It is not about restricting fun; it is about giving our fleets a shared identity worthy of the Dark Amarr.

Khanid Hulls

Vengeance, Anathema, Malediction, Heretic, Sacrilege, Curse, Damnation, Impel, and Bane remain the icons of the Kingdom: black, silver, missiles, and judgment.

Khanid & Cyber Knight SKINs

Cyber Knight SKINs for the Punisher are available for handout to KK members while supplies last. Other Khanid and Cyber Knight SKINs are commonly available in Amarr and Jita.

At-Cost Chapter SKINs

Khanid Karnivale makes Khanid SKINs at-cost for members, including standardized designs for many Navy Issue hulls. Pilots who want one should message Yukora Sae.

Chapter Artisan Registry

Yukora Sae

For custom Khanid SKIN requests, standardized Navy Issue liveries, or Chapter-approved visual schemes, contact Yukora Sae. The purpose is simple: make the fleet look like it belongs to the Kingdom before the first missile leaves the rack.
