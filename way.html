Doctrine

The Khanid Way

Tradition and adaptation. Beauty and violence. Good fights over meaningless statistics.

Independence

The Kingdom was born from refusal. We respect pilots who think, learn, and build themselves into something useful.

Competence Over Ego

Bring questions. Bring mistakes. Bring effort. Leave empty boasting at the undock.

Style With Purpose

Style is not an excuse to be useless. It is the final polish on a weapon that already works.

Real Life First

No alarm-clock CTAs. No mandatory misery. We are here to play, fight, laugh, and return again tomorrow.

Good Fights

Statistics are pleasant. Stories are better. We want fleets people remember.

Fly Khanid

Whenever possible, fly black, silver, green, beautiful, and dangerous.

Fly Dangerous. Fly Beautiful. Fly Khanid.
