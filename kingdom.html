The Kingdom

Khanid Kingdom lore, the Dark Amarr, and the rebellion that forged a sovereign power.

The Dark Amarr

The Khanid Kingdom, also known as the Dark Amarr, was founded a few centuries ago when the last Amarr Emperor was chosen. Khanid was one of the royal heirs at the time and, according to tradition, should have killed himself after failing to become emperor.

Khanid refused. Rather than submit to ritual death, he split his vast domains from the Amarr Empire and preserved his power by force of will.

War and Survival

The Empire retaliated, but it did not break the Kingdom. It conquered some vulnerable outer regions, yet the core of Khanid power endured. What survived was not a mere remnant, but a distinct civilization: Amarr in soul, Caldari in appetite for efficiency, and unmistakably Khanid in temperament.

A Surreal Fusion

The Kingdom still upholds many traditions of Amarr society while wholeheartedly embracing customs of others, mainly the Caldari. Many visitors describe it as a surreal mix of the Amarr and Caldari empires.

That is the Khanid miracle: faith, steel, profit, missiles, and monarchy — all sharpened into one dark imperial blade.
